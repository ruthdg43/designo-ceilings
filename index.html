At Designo Ceilings, we specialize in premium custom stretch ceilings and integrated LED lighting solutions that completely transform residential and commercial spaces. Our mission is simple: deliver a true wow factor through innovative design, precision fabrication, and flawless installation.

Our team brings deep European expertise to every project, inspired by modern ceiling systems and lighting designs from across Europe and Spain. Unlike traditional ceiling systems, we do not use drywall. Every ceiling is custom fabricated and engineered for performance, beauty, and longevity, changing the game for interior design in the United States.

All of our materials are fabricated in the United States, ensuring top tier quality, durability, and fast turnaround times. From sleek modern homes to large scale commercial environments, we work closely with custom home builders, architects, designers, and commercial contractors to deliver standout results.

We proudly serve Washington, DC, Virginia, and Maryland, providing fully customized ceiling solutions tailored to each space. No project is too small or too ambitious. If you can imagine it, we can build it.

To help clients visualize their space before installation, we also offer free custom renderings, giving a clear picture of how the new ceiling and lighting design will look once completed.

Whether someone is looking to elevate a home, office, retail space, or commercial property, Designo Ceilings is the trusted partner for next level custom ceilings.

Residential or commercial, feel free to give us a call at 703-775-1119 and let us design something extraordinary.
